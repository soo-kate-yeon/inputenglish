InputEnglish is an AI-powered shadowing app for English learners. Listen to native speakers and repeat after them to improve your speaking skills naturally.

Key Features:
- Shadowing Practice: Listen and repeat after native speakers
- AI Analysis: Get AI feedback on your pronunciation and intonation
- Listening Practice: Improve comprehension with diverse content
- Progress Tracking: Manage your learning journey systematically
- Personalized Learning: Content tailored to your level

What is Shadowing?
Shadowing is a language learning technique where you repeat what a native speaker says, like a shadow. It's one of the most effective methods for naturally acquiring pronunciation, intonation, and rhythm.
